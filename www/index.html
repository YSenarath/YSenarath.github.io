Yasas Senarath

Yasas is a graduate student at George Mason University. He is a member of the Humanitarian, Semantics & Informatics Lab (Human-Info-Lab). Prof. Hemant Purohit, the head of Human-Info-Lab advices Yasas on his research. He completed B.Sc in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Moratuwa in 2018.

His research interests lie in the area of Natural Language Processing and Artificial Intelligence. In his current research, he is aiming to provide solutions to problems in Natural and Societal Crises by analyzing social media content.
