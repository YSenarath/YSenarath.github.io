    Information Management System for Supermarkets

    2015

    Cooperative Society supermarkets (Co-op City) required an information management system to manage their sales and inventory. We developed a solution based on the requirement of Panadura Mega Co-op city, using Java which contains of subsystems; inventory management system, point of sales system, ledger management system and a customer management system. We followed Rational Unified Process (RUP) as the development process and carried out all the phases by ourselves. This project was carried out as the semester project of Object Oriented Software Development module in third semester at the university. The project won the best project title from 25 project presented at the end of the module.

    Product

    Eficiente

    2015

    An Arduino based project to design an automation system to conserve electrical energy and to remote control electrical appliances like bulbs and fans. The system detects absence of people in room and automatically shuts down power to electrical devices connected. The sensors automatically detect entrance of people to room and light up bulbs depending on the time of the day. In order enhance the capabilities of the system an Android based application was developed to control connected appliances manually via a Bluetooth enabled Android smartphone. Developed as semester project for semester 2, CS 1962: Engineering Skill Development module. Received an ‘A’ grade at final project evaluation.
